What I learned during class


LESSON 1: WHAT IS COMPUTING

• I now have a foundational understanding of the fundamental principles and concepts that constitute the field of computing.

LESSON 2: PROFESSIONS & CAREERS IN COMPUTING

• I am now equipped with knowledge about various professions and career paths within the computing industry, aiding me in making informed decisions about my future.

LESSON 3: INFORMATION PROCESSING CYCLE

• I can now comprehend the stages involved in the information processing cycle, enhancing my ability to analyze and optimize computing tasks.

LESSON 4: INTRODUCTION TO COMPUTER SYSTEMS

• I now have a comprehensive overview of computer systems, including hardware and software components, I now have a deeper understanding of how computers function.

LESSON 5: NUMBER SYSTEMS

• I now know the number systems used in computing, enhancing my ability to interpret and manipulate data effectively.

LESSON 6: BASIC CONCEPT OF COMPUTER SECURITY

• I now know the fundamental principles of computer security, enabling me to recognize potential vulnerabilities and employ basic protective measures.

LESSON 7: PROTECTING YOURSELF AGAINST CYBER ATTACKS

• I have developed skills to safeguard personal information and systems, empowering me to mitigate the risks associated with cyber threats.

LESSON 8: THE INTERNET & WORLD WIDE WEB

• I now know the fundamental workings of the Internet and the World Wide Web, enabling me to navigate and utilize online resources effectively.

LESSON 9: BASIC HTML & CSS

• I have acquired foundational skills in HTML and CSS, enabling me to create and style basic web pages independently.


Highlight

Despite being the most challenging, I find Lesson 9 Basic HTML & CSS to be my favorite. Although it can be overwhelming to navigate through unfamiliar coding territory at first, the sense of accomplishment that comes with understanding it is uniquely rewarding.
